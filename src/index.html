Game of Thrones, S2–S6

Queen Margaery Tyrell — daughter of Lord Mace Tyrell and Lady Alerie Tyrell, granddaughter of Lady Olenna, sister of Ser Loras. She became Queen Consort via marriage to King Joffrey Baratheon and later to King Tommen Baratheon. Accused of perjury amid the Sparrow movement, she perished when the Great Sept of Baelor was destroyed by wildfire in 303 AC.

  • Master of diplomacy and soft power.
  • Public works and charity earned her popular support.
  • Alliances often sealed with tact rather than force.

Biography

Raised in Highgarden, Margaery learned the arts of statecraft from her grandmother Olenna. In King’s Landing, she built public favor through charity and carefully curated appearances, balancing alliances with the Crown and the Faith.

Her story intertwines with the politics of Houses Tyrell and Baratheon. Ultimately, the destruction of the Great Sept of Baelor ended her life and the Tyrell bid for power.
